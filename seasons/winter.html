Welcome to the official Winter Page!

Winter is the FREEZING part of the year and it overlaps to the next year, usually a big Staple when it comes to the holidays as it has a couple big ones for many different religions.

Winter is associated with coats and snow, the temperature makes everything into an indoor activity. The snow makes the outside brighter than usual, when it comes to the cold, many animals migrate or hibernate. It turns into the hunting season and the perfect weather for people to enjoy inside with their warm hot cocoa and their warm fireplace huddled with their family.
